About Me

Born and raised in Smallville, Kansas, I moved to Metropolis to pursue my dream of becoming a journalist. At the Daily Bugle, I've had the privilege of covering some of the most significant stories of our time, from political investigations to extraordinary events that have shaped our understanding of what's possible.

My approach to journalism is rooted in compassion, integrity, and an unwavering belief that everyone deserves to have their story told. Whether I'm investigating corporate corruption or highlighting acts of everyday heroism, I strive to bring truth to light and give voice to those who need it most.
